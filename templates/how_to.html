How to Use ecom-tools

Step-by-step guide to reconcile Shopee orders and income

Step 1: Download Orders that you are interested to check

Preferably export one month of data.

  1. Log in to your Shopee Seller Center
  2. Go to Orders → My Orders
  3. In the My Orders page, click All
  4. Click Export
  5. Next, set the date to at least a month. Click Export afterwards. In this example, we downloaded all data for December 2025.
  6. Wait for the Shopee to finish then click the download button. This will be your Orders File. Save this to any directory available to you as we will need it later.

Step 2: Download Income Orders

Since we downloaded orders for December 2025, we extract at least two months of data from December 2025 to Jan 2026. This will take into account any Escrow Related Income

  1. Log in to your Shopee Seller Center
  2. Go to Finance → My Income Enter your password if prompted.
  3. Go to the Income Details and in the Released tab click Select Date
  4. Set the date to at least two months from the start of your Order date. This will take into account any Escrow Related Calculations
  5. Click the Export button and wait for it to finished.
  6. Wait for the Shopee to finish then click the Download button. This will be your Orders File

Step 3: Go back to the Home page and upload the two files.
